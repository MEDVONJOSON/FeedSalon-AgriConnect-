SMS Text Commands

Send any command below to: +232 77 XXX XXX

PRICE RICE BO

Get current rice prices in Bo District

Reply: Rice prices in Bo: Le 450,000/bag (50kg). Updated 2hrs ago.
WEATHER FREETOWN

Get 3-day weather forecast for Freetown

Reply: Freetown: Today 28°C, Rain 80%. Tomorrow 27°C, Cloudy. Sat 29°C, Sunny.
SELL CASSAVA 500KG KENEMA

List 500kg cassava for sale in Kenema

Reply: Listing created! Your cassava is now on marketplace. Ref: #A45821
TIP TOMATO

Get farming tip for tomatoes

Reply: Tomato Tip: Water early morning. Stake plants. Prune suckers. Watch for blight in rainy season.
