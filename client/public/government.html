Available Government Programs 2025

Rice Seed Subsidy Program

Active

50% subsidy on certified rice seeds for smallholder farmers

Eligibility: Farmers with < 5 hectares
Subsidy Amount: Up to Le 500,000 per farmer
Deadline: June 30, 2025

Fertilizer Support Scheme

Active

40% discount on NPK and Urea fertilizers

Eligibility: All registered farmers
Subsidy Amount: Up to Le 750,000 per season
Deadline: December 31, 2025
